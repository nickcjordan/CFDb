About the graybeards

Team Bio: The Graybeards was originally founded by Nick and David long before computer science was on their minds, as they were part of a group project for a business foundations class years ago. Soon after, they started taking CS courses, and a study group began to emerge. It wasn't long before The Graybeards had a large number of members. The group decided to expand their name to "The Graybeard Society of Computer Science Gentlemen," but keep "The Graybeards" as a shorter nickname. Time has passed, and the group now has over 10 members, and continues to expand. Many members of The Graybeards are not featured on this website, but every member of this group is a Graybeard.

Data:

http://www.cfbdatawarehouse.com/

http://www.cfbstats.com/

http://www.wikipedia.com/fbs

Description: The player stats were scraped from cfbstats and the team stats were scraped from cfbdatawarehouse. Both sources of data were harvested using web scraping with the Python library Beautiful Soup by iterating through each team's and player's webpages to grab all the needed stats out of the HTML source code. Wikipedia was used for additional, small amounts of outlier data that was needed.
